How does VoteMe works?

by Sri Lanka Institute of Information Technology

Posted on July 17, 2022

Standing in the queue and waiting for your turn to cast vote is a part of a history now.

Sri Lanka Institute of Information Technology proposes a new system of voting where everything is transparent and no question of tampering the voting machine as it doesn't exists in first place.

VoteMe revolutionizes the voting process by securely storing all votes in the Blockchain, ensuring an incorruptible and transparent system.

This is still under various trials! Please expect few bugs.

How to use?

1. Install MetaMask Browser Extension(This Extension only support for Chrome and Firefox).

2. Make sure that the Ethereum Account which you've been assigned is imported into MetaMask.

3. Visit the Cast Vote Page and cast your vote
